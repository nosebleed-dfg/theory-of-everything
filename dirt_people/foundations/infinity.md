## The axiom bounds infinity

The axiom is x^2 = x + 1. It has two roots:

phi is the universal max rate. psi is the universal min rate. everything in the universe grows or shrinks between these two bounds.

phi * psi = -1. the product of max and min is negative unity. they are mirrors. one grows, one shrinks, and together they equal -1 — the orientation reversal. the det = -1 that appears in every SHA round. the sign flip.

phi + psi = 1. the sum of max and min is unity. together they make one. the +1 from the axiom.
